Suzuki Swift R

Picture from mynewsdesk.com

The most iconic car from Suzuki. The Suzuki Swift.

The Suzuki Swift RS is the rally version of the Suzuki Swift, which debuted in the Championship on 2018 in the R1 category. The little Swift would partecipate also at the Suzuki Rally Trophy and Suzuki Rally Cup, together with the Swift Sport 1600 R1.

The Boosterjet engine of the Swift

The Suzuki Swift RS, has a 1.0l turbo engine with 3 cylinders, five gears
